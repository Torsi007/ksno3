Kitesurfing.no has been running kite courses on Jæren since 2001. Through these courses we've obtained a lot of experience which make us one of the best kiteschools in Norway.
Our business focus soley on course activities, wich enables us to be flexible in our lessons. We customize our courses to meet the expectations of our customers while preserving what matters most: Safety and having fun!
KSNO is an established kiteschool who strive to ensure a safe, fun and exciting environment to learn and progress in kitesurfing!
